Case Presentation Structure

A total of **10 cases** will be presented, with **5 cases submitted from Taiwan** and **5 from Japan**[cite: 19].

Each case will have a **15-minute discussion** period, divided into three 5-minute segments[cite: 20]:

  • **5 Minutes:** Case responder presents their diagnosis of the case[cite: 21].
  • **5 Minutes:** Case submitter presents the original diagnosis[cite: 22].
  • **5 Minutes:** Moderator discusses the case[cite: 23].
